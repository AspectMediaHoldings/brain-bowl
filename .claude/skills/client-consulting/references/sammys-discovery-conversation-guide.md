## Before the Meeting

### What to Bring
- [ ] This guide (printed or on phone/tablet)
- [ ] Notebook for additional notes
- [ ] Phone to take photos of any materials he has
- [ ] Your laptop (optional - to show examples)

### Meeting Setup
- **Best Location:** His office or shop (you might find marketing materials, photos, awards)
- **Time Needed:** 45-60 minutes
- **Tone:** Casual conversation, not an interrogation. You're helping a friend.

### Set Expectations

> *I've got some questions to go through, but don't worry - it's more of a conversation. Some stuff you'll know off the top of your head, some things we might need to figure out together. Sound good?"*

**Why this matters:** Sets a collaborative tone, shows you're thinking about ROI not just design.
