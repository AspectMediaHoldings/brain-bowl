# STEP 2: Create Free Canva Account (2 min)

1. Go to www.canva.com

2. Click "Sign up" (top right)

3. Sign up with email or Google

4. Choose FREE plan (you don't need Pro!)

5. Skip all tutorial popups

# STEP 3: Create Your First Mockup - Main Bundle (10 min)

## The Process:

1. Click "Create a design" (top right in Canva)

2. Select "Custom Size" → Enter: 2000 x 2000 pixels

3. Click "Create new design"

4. In left sidebar, search: "planner mockup"

5. Look for templates showing 3 pages/tablets side by side

6. Click one you like - it adds to your canvas

7. Drag corners to resize it to fill canvas

8. Click on the FIRST mock page in template

9. Click "Uploads" in left sidebar

10. Upload your scout-tracker screenshot

11. Click the uploaded image - it replaces the placeholder

12. Click on SECOND mock page

13. Upload and replace with Camping Planner screenshot

14. Click on THIRD mock page

15. Upload and replace with Activity Planner screenshot

16. Add text heading:

• Click "T" (Text) in left sidebar

• Add heading: "Complete Scout Planning Bundle"

• Make it BIG and BOLD (try 72pt+)

• Choose forest green or navy blue color

17. Download your mockup:

• Click "Share" button (top right)

• Click "Download"

• Choose "JPG"

• Click Download button

• Save as: "1-scout-bundle-main.jpg"

# PRO TIPS FOR AMAZING MOCKUPS

## ✅ Color Scheme:

• Use Scout colors: forest green, brown, navy blue, gold

• Avoid bright pink, purple - doesn't match theme

• Max 2-3 colors per mockup

## ✅ Text Overlays:

• Make text LARGE - people scroll fast on phones

• High contrast (dark on light or light on dark)

• Bold fonts only: Arial Black, Montserrat Bold

• Keep SHORT: 5-8 words maximum

## ✅ Screenshot Quality:

• Ensure PDF text is sharp and readable

• Crop out blank white space

• Use pages that look "full" of content

## ✅ Consistency:

• Use similar mockup styles for all images

• Same color scheme across all

• Same font family throughout

• Creates professional, cohesive brand

# COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

❌ Using too many different mockup styles

✓ Pick 1-2 templates and reuse them

❌ Text too small to read

✓ If you can't read it on phone, make it bigger

❌ Cluttered, busy backgrounds

✓ Clean, simple backgrounds = product stands out

❌ Blurry screenshots

✓ High-res screenshots, zoom out when capturing

❌ Forgetting to save/download

✓ Download each mockup as you finish

❌ Using Canva Pro features (adds watermark)

✓ Only use FREE templates (no crown icon)
