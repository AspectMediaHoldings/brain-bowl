# AI Video Platform Evaluation

## Purpose

Aspect Media Holdings, LLC evaluates AI video generation platforms on a defined monthly
budget to support Dogs & Coffee and other product line promotions. This skill governs
the evaluation process: research, criteria scoring, comparison, recommendation, and
quarterly refresh. Meghan operates the tools. Sarah tracks costs in QuickBooks.

## When to Use

- Evaluating a new AI video platform for Aspect Media use
- Updating a prior platform comparison report
- Answering "is this platform worth the cost" for a specific tool
- Advising on monthly or annual subscription decisions for video tools
- Producing a quarterly platform review for the team

## Inputs

Confirm before starting:
1. Platform(s) to evaluate: specific names, or open research
2. Monthly budget ceiling — verify current amount with Nathan before recommending subscriptions
3. Primary use case: TikTok/Reels short-form, Etsy product videos, paid ad creation, or all three
4. Who will operate the tool: Nathan, Meghan, or both
5. Whether this is a new evaluation or an update to a prior report

## Procedure

### Evaluation Criteria (score each platform on all five)

Apply these criteria in priority order:

1. **Output quality for POD lifestyle content**
   - Does it produce credible lifestyle/product footage for apparel brands?
   - Is the output suitable for paid advertising (higher standard than organic)?
   - Score: Poor / Acceptable / Good / Excellent

2. **Cost per video vs. monthly budget**
   - What is the cost per generated video or per minute of output?
   - Does the subscription include commercial licensing for paid ads?
   - Is the commitment monthly or annual? Flag annual commitments clearly.
   - Score against budget ceiling: Over budget / At limit / Under budget with headroom

3. **Ease of use for non-technical operators**
   - Meghan is the primary operator. Assume no video production background required.
   - Is the interface prompt-based or does it require complex project setup?
   - Score: Complex / Moderate / Simple

4. **Turnaround time**
   - How long from prompt to exported video?
   - Is it async (wait for render) or real-time?
   - Score: Hours / 30+ min / Under 30 min / Near-instant

5. **Output format compatibility**
   - Does it export vertical 9:16 for TikTok/Reels?
   - Does it export horizontal 16:9 for website use?
   - What resolution? Minimum acceptable: 1080p.
   - Score: Limited / Adequate / Full compatibility

### Research Process

1. Search for current pricing and features — platforms change frequently.
2. Check for a free trial or limited free tier to test before committing.
3. Verify commercial use licensing for paid advertising — this is non-negotiable.
4. Look for user reviews specific to product/apparel/POD use cases.
5. Check turnaround time from user reports, not just marketing claims.

### Report Structure

Produce the evaluation as a structured document:

1. **Executive summary** — one paragraph: what was evaluated, top recommendation, reason.
2. **Comparison table** — one row per platform, one column per criterion.
3. **Platform detail sections** — one section per platform with narrative notes.
4. **Recommendation** — single ranked recommendation. State the runner-up. No ambiguous "it depends" conclusion.
5. **Budget allocation** — recommended monthly spend for the winning platform(s).
6. **Next review date** — quarterly default.

Do not deliver a report without a clear single recommendation. If two platforms are genuinely tied, state which to start with and why.

### Quarterly Refresh

Each quarter:
1. Check for pricing or feature changes on the current active platform.
2. Check for new entrants in the AI video space.
3. Confirm the current platform still fits the budget.
4. Update the comparison table.
5. Deliver a one-page update to Nathan, not a full re-evaluation unless a platform change is recommended.

## Output Format

- Evaluation report: Word document (.docx) or PDF, comparison table + narrative + recommendation.
- Quarterly update: one-page Word document or plain text, table + change notes + recommendation confirmation.
- Quick answer (single platform question): three-to-five sentence assessment, score on all five criteria, go/no-go recommendation.

## Quality Checks

Before delivering any report:
- Confirm commercial ad licensing is verified for the recommended platform.
- Confirm monthly budget ceiling has been applied to all recommendations.
- Confirm annual commitments are flagged clearly.
- Confirm a single recommendation is stated — not a list of equal options.
- Confirm pricing is current (search before finalizing — do not use training data for pricing).
