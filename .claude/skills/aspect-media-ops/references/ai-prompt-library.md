# SECTION 8: USAGE TIPS & BEST PRACTICES

## Prompt Customization Guide

### Making Prompts More Specific

**Original:**
"Create a birthday card with flowers"

**Better:**
"Create a 5x7 inch vertical birthday card with soft watercolor pink roses arranged in bottom right corner, white background, romantic style"

**Best:**
"Create a 5x7 inch vertical birthday card design with soft watercolor pink roses. The roses should be arranged in the bottom right corner with delicate green leaves. Use a white background with subtle watercolor texture. The design should have plenty of white space at the top and center for text. Color palette: soft pink, blush, sage green, cream white. Style: romantic, elegant, feminine, hand-painted watercolor effect. NO TEXT in the image."

---

### Color Specification Tips

**Vague:** "Use pink"  
**Better:** "Use soft pink and blush"  
**Best:** "Use soft pink (#FFB6C1), blush, sage green (#87AE73), cream (#FFFDD0)"

**ChatGPT:** Artistic color names work well  
**Gemini:** Hex codes improve accuracy significantly  
**Grok:** Simple color names are fine

---

### Placement Precision

**Vague:** "Flowers in corner"  
**Better:** "Flowers in bottom right corner"  
**Best:** "Roses arranged in bottom right corner with space at top and center for text overlay"

**Key phrases for placement:**
- "bottom right corner"
- "along the left edge"
- "centered at bottom"
- "top right flowing downward"
- "scattered across bottom third"
- "vertical border on left side"
- "horizontal border at bottom"

---

**For Watercolor:**
- hand-painted

- artistic interpretation

**For Minimalist:**

**For Veteran/Military:**

### Background Instructions

**Always specify:**
- Background color
- Texture (if any)

**Good examples:**
- "white background with subtle watercolor texture"
- "pure white background, completely clean"
- "cream background with soft gradient"
- "aged paper beige background"
- "soft pastel pink background"

**Always end prompts with:**
- "NO TEXT in the image."
- "No typography or text elements."
- "No words or text in the image."

**If platform adds text anyway:**
- Regenerate 2-3 times
- Add to prompt: "The image must be completely text-free with no words, letters, or typography visible"
- Try different platform

## Prompt Troubleshooting

**Problem: Image is too busy**

**Solution:**
- Add "with plenty of white space"
- Add "simple and minimal"
- Reduce number of elements in prompt

---

**Problem: Colors don't match**

**Solution:**
- Add hex codes (Gemini especially)
- Add "EXACTLY these colors"
- Emphasize color palette twice in prompt

---

**Problem: Wrong placement**

**Solution:**
- Be more specific: "positioned in lower right corner"
- Add "with significant space in upper left for text"
- Include measurements: "covering bottom third only"

---

**Problem: Too photographic (wanted artistic)**

**Solution:**
- Emphasize "watercolor" or "illustrated"
- Add "painterly" or "hand-painted"
- Add "NOT photorealistic"

---

**Problem: Too illustrated (wanted realistic)**

**Solution:**
- Emphasize "photorealistic" or "realistic"
- Use Gemini or Grok (better at realism)
- Add "photograph-style" or "high detail"
