# PINTEREST SETUP (5 Minutes)

## Step 1: Create Business Account

1. Go to pinterest.com/business/create

2. Sign up with email

3. Choose "Publishing" or "Online Retail" as business type

4. Add profile photo (logo or Scout-themed image)

5. Bio: "Scout planning printables & camping resources for Troop leaders & families | Digital downloads"

## Step 2: Create Boards

Create these boards:

## Step 3: Claim Your Etsy Shop

1. Go to Pinterest Settings > Claim

# HOW TO CREATE PINS IN CANVA

## Pinterest Pin Size: 1000 x 1500 pixels (vertical)

Quick Steps:

1. In Canva, create new design: 1000 x 1500 pixels

2. Use your existing mockup images

3. Resize them to fit vertical format

4. Add large, bold text overlay at top

5. Keep design simple - Pinterest users scroll FAST

6. Use bright, eye-catching colors

7. Make sure text is readable on phone

## Template Shortcut:

• Search "Pinterest Pin" in Canva templates

• Pick a simple, clean template

• Replace images with your mockups

• Change text to match pin ideas above

• Create 5-10 pins in 30 minutes!
