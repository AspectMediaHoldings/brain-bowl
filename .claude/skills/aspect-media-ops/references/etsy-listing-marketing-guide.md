# CREATING PRODUCT MOCKUPS (CRITICAL!)

You need 5-10 product photos for your Etsy listing. Here's how to create them for FREE:

## Method 1: Canva Mockups (Easiest)

1. Open Canva (free account)

2. Search 'iPad mockup' or 'tablet mockup' or 'planner mockup'

3. Choose a template

4. Upload screenshots of your PDF pages

5. Replace the template image with your screenshot

6. Download as JPG

## Method 2: Physical Print Photos

1. Print 2-3 pages of each planner

2. Take photos on a clean desk/table with natural light

3. Include props: pens, coffee mug, Scout handbook

4. Use your phone camera - good lighting is key!

5. Edit slightly to brighten/enhance
