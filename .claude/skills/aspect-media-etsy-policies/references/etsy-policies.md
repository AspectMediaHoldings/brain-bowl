## Section 1: Return and Exchange Policy

All products are made to order. No inventory held. Each item produced only after order is placed.

### What We Cover (contact within 30 days of delivery)
- Print defects: fading, bleeding, misalignment, color shift
- Wrong item shipped
- Damage during shipping
- Size or color different from what was ordered

Process: Customer sends photo to Etsy inbox. We arrange free reprint or full refund — customers choice.

### What We Do Not Cover
- Returns for buyers remorse or change of mind
- Wrong size selected by customer at checkout
- Orders shipped to incorrect address provided by customer

### Cancellations
Contact immediately. Once an order enters production (typically within 24 hours of purchase), cancellation is not possible.

## Section 3: Etsy Shop Policy Fields (ready to paste)

### Returns and Exchanges
All items are made to order. We do not accept returns or exchanges for buyers remorse or wrong size selected at checkout. If your order arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, contact us within 30 days of delivery with a photo of the issue. We will send a free replacement or issue a full refund. Your choice. We ship to the address you provide at checkout. Please verify your address before placing your order. We are not responsible for orders shipped to an incorrect address entered by the customer.

### Shipping and Processing
Every item is printed and shipped by our production partner after you order. We do not ship from our own stock. Production time: 2-4 business days. Shipping time: 3-7 business days after production. Total estimated delivery: 5-11 business days from purchase. We currently ship within the United States only. Tracking information will be sent to your Etsy account when your order ships.

### Payments
We accept all major credit cards and PayPal through Etsys secure checkout. Payment is collected at the time of purchase.

### Custom and Personalized Orders
We do not currently offer custom designs or personalized orders. All designs are listed as shown.

### FAQ
- Can I cancel? Contact us immediately. Once in production, cancellation is not possible.
- Can I exchange for a different size? No. Review the size chart before ordering.
- Order shows delivered but not received? Contact your local carrier first, then message us.
- When will my order ship? Production takes 2-4 business days. You will receive a tracking number when it ships.
