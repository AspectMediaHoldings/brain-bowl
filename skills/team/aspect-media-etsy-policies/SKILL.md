# Aspect Media Etsy Shop Policies

## Purpose

Aspect Media Holdings, LLC operates multiple Etsy storefronts under a shared policy
framework: Dogs & Coffee, State Bird Emblems, and Family Matching Shirts. This skill
governs all policy language — returns, exchanges, fulfillment disclosures, cancellations,
and customer-facing FAQ scripts. Policies must be trademark-safe, Etsy-compliant, and
consistently applied across all three shops.

## When to Use

- Drafting or updating a return and exchange policy for any Aspect Media Etsy shop
- Writing a fulfillment disclosure (POD processing and shipping times)
- Producing a cancellation policy block
- Creating seasonal holiday order deadline language
- Drafting FAQ scripts for customer service scenarios
- Reviewing existing policy language for compliance or completeness

## Inputs

Confirm before starting:
1. Which shop is in scope: Dogs & Coffee, State Bird Emblems, Family Matching Shirts, or all three
2. Task type: new policy draft, update existing, or seasonal swap (holiday block)
3. Whether this is for internal reference, Etsy shop settings, or listing description embed
4. Any recent Etsy policy changes that may affect the template (search if uncertain)

If shop is unspecified, produce language applicable to all three shops.

## Procedure

### Return and Exchange Policy

Aspect Media backs:
- Items with print defects or production errors
- Wrong item shipped (wrong design, wrong size due to our error)
- Items damaged in shipping (require photo evidence)

Aspect Media does not cover:
- Customer remorse (ordered and changed mind)
- Wrong size ordered by the customer
- Incorrect shipping address provided by the customer
- Items lost in transit beyond 30 days without tracking evidence

Policy language must be direct and specific. Do not use vague softeners like "we will do our best." State exactly what is and is not covered.

Always include: how to contact us (Etsy message), what information to include (order number, photo of defect), and the resolution offered (replacement or refund — not both as an open choice; specify which applies).

### Fulfillment Disclosure

All Aspect Media products are made-to-order through Printify.

Standard disclosure language:
- Production time: 2-4 business days
- Shipping time: 3-7 business days (domestic US)
- Total fulfillment: 5-11 business days
- No inventory is held — each item is produced per order

Provide two versions:
1. Short (one line): for tight listing spaces and shop announcements
2. Full paragraph: for listing descriptions and About section

### Cancellation Policy

Orders may be cancelled within 24 hours of purchase before entering production.
After 24 hours, the order has likely entered production and cannot be cancelled.
Do not promise cancellations after the 24-hour window.

### Holiday Order Deadline Block

Produce a seasonal swap block that Nathan can drop into the shop announcement.
Include: last order date for guaranteed pre-holiday delivery, a note that production
and shipping add 5-11 days, and a recommendation to order early.

Update this block with specific dates when Nathan provides the target holiday.

### Customer FAQ Scripts

Produce scripts for these four scenarios — all are common and must be ready:

1. **Cancellation request after 24 hours**
   - Acknowledge the request promptly.
   - Explain production has started and cancellation is not possible.
   - Offer to help with any other issue.

2. **Wrong size — customer error**
   - Acknowledge clearly.
   - Explain that sizing charts are provided in the listing.
   - Offer to assist with an exchange at cost (customer pays return shipping and difference).
   - Do not offer a free replacement for customer-ordered wrong size.

3. **Missing package (in transit)**
   - Ask for order number and shipping address confirmation.
   - Check tracking status.
   - If beyond estimated delivery window: open a case with the carrier before issuing replacement.
   - Do not issue a replacement until the carrier case is filed.

4. **Print quality complaint**
   - Request a clear photo of the defect alongside the item.
   - If defect is confirmed: offer replacement at no charge, ship to same address.
   - Route to Printify reprint process — do not issue a cash refund unless Printify denies the reprint.

## Output Format

- Policy documents: Word document (.docx) with labeled sections per Etsy policy field.
- Listing embeds: plain text blocks, clearly labeled as "short" or "full paragraph."
- FAQ scripts: numbered list, one script per scenario, customer-facing language only.
- Holiday blocks: plain text, dated and labeled for the specific holiday.

## Quality Checks

Before delivering any policy document:
- Confirm coverage and non-coverage are both stated explicitly — no ambiguous "may" language.
- Confirm 24-hour cancellation window is specified.
- Confirm fulfillment disclosure includes both production and shipping times separately.
- Confirm FAQ scripts do not promise free replacements for customer-error scenarios.
- Confirm language is consistent across all three shops (if producing for all).
