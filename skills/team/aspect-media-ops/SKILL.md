## Product Line Reference

### Dogs and Coffee

**Niche:** Pet products — dog-specific items
**Tone:** Casual and warm
**Market context:** The pet POD niche carries profit margins of 45-65% on Etsy.
Dog-specific products (bandanas, food mats, blankets, custom portraits) are top
performers. AI-personalized pet products are among the most profitable POD niches
in 2026. The Dogs and Coffee line may be underleveraging personalization — this is
an active strategic consideration.
**Active research task:** Etsy keyword volume research for Dogs and Coffee is pending.
Use Everbee for keyword and revenue estimates.

### State Bird Emblems

**Niche:** Regional pride, nature-themed
**Tone:** Regional and pride-driven
**Market context:** This line has natural home decor placement potential. Home decor
is the fastest-growing POD category (projected 24.2% CAGR). Listings or products
that cross into home decor (prints, pillows, wall art) are worth testing.

### Family Matching Shirts

**Niche:** Family apparel, occasion-driven
**Tone:** Family-focused and celebratory
**Market context:** Strongest seasonal peaks around Mother's Day, Father's Day,
Thanksgiving, Christmas, and family vacations. The "coordinated sets" concept —
products designed to work together — is trending on Etsy in 2026. Plan catalog
and listing work to lead these windows by 6-8 weeks.

## Procedure

1. Confirm the product line and platform before doing any work.
2. Confirm whether output is internal or external.
3. For external output: apply the pre-delivery check and wait for Nathan's explicit
   approval before treating anything as publishable or client-ready.
4. For design direction involving Meghan: describe the request clearly with product
   line and platform context. Note relevant Etsy trend data if applicable. Do not
   make final design decisions.
5. For financial questions: surface the question and the data. Do not interpret or
   conclude. Flag for Sarah.
6. For listing copy or product descriptions: match the product line tone (see Product
   Line Reference above). Reference current Etsy trend data where relevant.

   directing him to run a search.

   window is approaching and prep work has not started.
