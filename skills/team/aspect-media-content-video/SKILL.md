# Aspect Media POD Social Video Production

## Purpose

Aspect Media Holdings, LLC produces short-form social video content to promote its
print-on-demand brands — primarily Dogs & Coffee. This skill governs music selection,
editing tool guidance, platform strategy, and AI video platform evaluation for TikTok,
Reels, Etsy product listings, and paid ads. Output must be royalty-free, commercially
licensed, and optimized per platform.

## When to Use

- Selecting music for a Dogs & Coffee or other Aspect Media promotional video
- Advising on CapCut editing workflow for vertical social content
- Developing platform-specific video strategy (TikTok vs. Etsy vs. paid ads)
- Evaluating AI video generation platforms against the monthly budget
- Producing a search string list for royalty-free music platforms
- Any short-form video content task under Aspect Media Holdings

## Inputs

Confirm before starting:
1. Product line: Dogs & Coffee, State Bird Emblems, Family Matching Shirts, or other
2. Platform target: TikTok/Reels, Etsy listing, paid ads, or multi-platform
3. Video length and orientation (default: 10-30 seconds, vertical)
4. Vibe or aesthetic: upbeat/trendy, chill/relaxed, Americana, lifestyle, other
5. Whether the task is music selection, editing guidance, platform strategy, or platform evaluation

## Procedure

### Music Selection

Determine platform context first — this drives the music strategy.

**TikTok and paid ads (attention-capture):** Select upbeat, trend-aligned tracks.
Goal is to stop the scroll in the first two seconds. Prioritize energy and hook speed.

**Etsy listings and website embeds (engagement):** Select chill, relaxed indie tracks.
Viewer is already interested. Music should feel like a lifestyle accessory, not an interruption.

Do not use the same track across all platforms. Optimize per context.

**Music must be:**
- Royalty-free
- Licensed for commercial use (covers paid advertising)
- Cleared for TikTok, Instagram/Reels, and Etsy

**Approved sourcing platforms:**
- Epidemic Sound (primary — subscription, full commercial clearance)
- Artlist (subscription, full commercial clearance including ads)
- Uppbeat (free tier available, verify commercial license per track)
- Pixabay (free, verify commercial license per track)

When delivering music recommendations, provide:
1. Copy-paste search strings per platform
2. Two directions: upbeat (social/ads) and chill (product listings)
3. License confirmation: "commercial use including paid ads" — verify per platform's terms

### CapCut Editing Guidance

CapCut is the primary editing tool. It has no watermark and is optimized for TikTok format.

Standard workflow:
1. Import video clip(s)
2. Add selected audio track (import from device or use CapCut's library for platform-cleared tracks)
3. Trim to target length — default 10-30 seconds for social
4. Add text overlay only if needed (keep minimal)
5. Export: 1080x1920 (vertical), MP4, highest resolution available
6. Do not use CapCut's built-in music for paid advertising — it is not cleared for commercial ad use.

### AI Video Platform Evaluation

When evaluating platforms for Aspect Media:

Evaluation criteria (in priority order):
1. Output quality for lifestyle/product POD content
2. Cost per video relative to monthly budget
3. Ease of use (Meghan and Nathan are primary operators)
4. Turnaround time
5. Platform output format compatibility (vertical 9:16, horizontal 16:9)

Deliverable: structured comparison table in Word or PDF format.
Include: platform name, cost, output quality rating, ease of use rating, turnaround, recommendation.
Conclude with a single ranked recommendation and budget allocation for the quarter.
Flag platforms that require annual commitment vs. monthly.

## Output Format

- Music recommendations: bulleted list with copy-paste search strings, organized by platform and vibe.
- Editing guides: numbered step-by-step in Word or plain text.
- Platform evaluations: Word document (.docx) or PDF, comparison table + narrative recommendation.
- Social strategy notes: short bullet list, platform-specific, no more than one page.

All output uses active voice, short sentences, no filler vocabulary.

## Quality Checks

Before delivering music recommendations:
- Confirm all recommended sources allow commercial use including paid advertising.
- Confirm two tracks or directions are provided (upbeat and chill).
- Confirm copy-paste search strings are included — not just platform names.

Before delivering platform evaluation:
- Confirm monthly budget constraint is applied.
- Confirm evaluation covers all five criteria.
- Confirm a single clear recommendation is stated, not a list of equal options.

Before delivering editing guidance:
- Confirm CapCut built-in music is flagged as not cleared for paid ads.
- Confirm export specs are stated (1080x1920, MP4).
