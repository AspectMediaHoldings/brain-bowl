# PART 3: FACELESS MARKETING STRATEGY

## What Is Faceless Marketing?

Creating and promoting digital products without showing your face or revealing your identity. Focus is on the product, not the personality.

### Best Platforms for Faceless Marketing:

1. PINTEREST (Highest ROI for digital products)

• Creates evergreen traffic

• Pins stay visible for months

• Visual search engine perfect for templates/planners

2. INSTAGRAM (Reels & Stories)

• Use product mockups and flat lays

• Text overlay tutorials

• Behind-the-scenes of your designs

3. TIKTOK (Short-form content)

• 'How I use this planner' videos

• Screen recordings of templates

• AI voiceovers for explanations

4. ETSY (Primary sales platform)

• Built-in buyer traffic

• No face needed in listings

• Focus on product photos

### Content Ideas (No Face Required):

✓ Product mockups and demonstrations

✓ 'Unboxing' style videos of your digital products

✓ Text-based tips and tutorials

✓ Before/after comparisons

✓ Customer testimonials (screenshots)

✓ Process videos (screen recordings)

✓ Aesthetic product photos/flat lays

✓ Stats and infographics about your niche

### Essential Tools (All Free/Low Cost):

Design: Canva (free tier is excellent)

PDF Creation: LibreOffice or Canva

Video Editing: CapCut (free)

Mockups: Placeit or Canva mockups

Scheduling: Later or Buffer (free plans)

AI Voiceovers: ElevenLabs (free tier)

# PART 4: YOUR 90-DAY ACTION PLAN

## MONTH 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

Week 1: Research & Setup

• Choose your niche based on your skills/interests

• Study top sellers on Etsy in your niche

• Create Etsy shop and social media accounts

• Set up Canva and learn basic tools

Week 2-3: Create Your First 5 Products

• Start with templates or planners (easiest)

• Focus on quality over quantity

• Ensure products solve specific problems

• Create product mockups

Week 4: Launch & Initial Marketing

• List all 5 products on Etsy

• Optimize titles, tags, descriptions for SEO

• Create 20 Pinterest pins

• Post 3-5 times per week on Instagram/TikTok

## MONTH 2: Expand Catalog (Weeks 5-8)

• Create 10-15 more products (aim for 20 total)

• Analyze which products get views/favorites

• Create bundles of your best products

• Build content library (50+ social posts)

• Engage with customers and answer questions

• Start email list if possible

## MONTH 3: Scale & Optimize (Weeks 9-12)

• Reach 30-50 product listings

• Double down on what's selling

• Create variations of bestsellers

• Consider paid ads (small budget)

• Build systems for consistent posting

• Aim for first regular sales

# PART 6: PRICING STRATEGY

## Sweet Spot Pricing:

• Single templates: $3-8

• Comprehensive planners: $8-15

• Bundles/e-books: $15-30

• Premium packages: $30-50

### Pricing Psychology:

• Start lower to build reviews (social proof)

• Increase prices as you get 20+ reviews

• Bundle 3-5 items for 'better value' perception

• Use .99 endings ($14.99 vs $15.00)

• Create tiered pricing (basic/premium versions)

# PART 7: SEO & MARKETING ESSENTIALS

## Etsy SEO Critical Points:

• Use all 13 tags - be specific

• Include main keyword in title first

• Write detailed descriptions (300+ words)

• Use high-quality mockup images

• Show the product being used

• Include 'what's included' section

## Pinterest Strategy (CRITICAL for passive traffic):

• Create 3-5 pins per product

• Use vertical format (1000x1500px)

• Text overlay with clear benefit

• Link directly to Etsy listing

• Pin consistently (5-10 per day scheduled)

• Join relevant group boards

## Social Media Content Calendar:

Wednesday: Customer testimonial/review

Thursday: Behind the scenes

Friday: Tips related to your niche

Weekend: Inspirational/motivational content
