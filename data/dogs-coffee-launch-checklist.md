================================================================
  DOGS & COFFEE LLC — BUSINESS LAUNCH CHECKLIST
  Spring 2026 Go-Live
================================================================

[ ] 01 — GET EIN FROM THE IRS
       Apply online at irs.gov — free and instant.
       You will need your EIN for everything else on this list.
       URL: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online

----------------------------------------------------------------

[ ] 02 — PRINT ARTICLES OF ORGANIZATION & LLC OPERATING AGREEMENT
       Download from Sunbiz.org.
       Print both — banks and the county will want physical copies.
       URL: https://dos.fl.gov/sunbiz/

----------------------------------------------------------------

[ ] 03 — OPEN LLC BANK ACCOUNT — ADD SARAH AS AUTHORIZED SIGNER
       Bring to the bank:
         - Articles of Organization
         - LLC Operating Agreement
         - EIN confirmation letter from IRS
         - Government-issued photo ID (driver's license)
         - Secondary ID (Social Security card, utility bill, etc.)
         - Initial deposit
       Call ahead to confirm whether Sarah must be present at opening.

----------------------------------------------------------------

[ ] 04 — REGISTER WITH FLORIDA DEPT. OF REVENUE (SALES TAX — DR-1)
       File Form DR-1 online.
       You will receive a Certificate of Registration and
       Annual Resale Certificate upon approval.
       URL: https://taxapps.floridarevenue.com/taxregistration

----------------------------------------------------------------

[ ] 05 — REGISTER WITH JACKSON COUNTY TAX COLLECTOR
         (LOCAL BUSINESS TAX RECEIPT)
       Visit in person: 4125 Jireh Ct, Marianna, FL 32448
       Bring Articles of Organization, EIN, and photo ID.
       URL: https://jacksontc.com

----------------------------------------------------------------

[ ] 06 — PURCHASE QUICKBOOKS SUBSCRIPTION
       Recommended: QuickBooks Plus (required for Class Tracking
       to run separate P&L per product line).
       URL: https://quickbooks.intuit.com

----------------------------------------------------------------

[ ] 07 — SET UP QUICKBOOKS & CONNECT BANK ACCOUNT
       Link the LLC bank account in QuickBooks.
       Set up chart of accounts, classes, and products/services
       per the QuickBooks Setup Guide.

----------------------------------------------------------------

[ ] 08 — CONTRIBUTE INITIAL CAPITAL TO LLC (MEGAN & NATHAN)
       Transfer funds from both members into the LLC bank account.
       Record each as a Capital Contribution in QuickBooks —
       NOT as income.
         - Nathan: Account 3010 — Nathan Capital Contributions
         - Megan:  Account 3110 — Megan Capital Contributions

----------------------------------------------------------------

[ ] 09 — START PRINTIFY & ETSY — LINK TO QUICKBOOKS
       Create Printify and Etsy accounts under the LLC.
       Connect both to QuickBooks via app integrations or A2X
       for clean transaction tracking.
       URL: https://printify.com
       URL: https://sell.etsy.com
       A2X integration: https://a2xaccounting.com

================================================================
  NOTES
================================================================

  - Complete items in order. EIN must come first.
  - Bank account must be open before QuickBooks can be
    connected or capital contributed.
  - DR-1 and County registration can be done same week
    as bank account opening.
  - See QB Setup Guide and Month-End Close SOP for full
    QuickBooks configuration and bookkeeping procedures.
